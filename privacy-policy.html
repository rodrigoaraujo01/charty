Charty Privacy Policy

Charty was developed by Rodrigo Araújo as a freemium app. This app is provided through Apple's App Store at no cost and is intended for use as is.

Data storage

Charty does not collect nor share any data from its users. All data provided to Charty is stored locally using Apple's CoreData container. In order to keep data in sync between your devices, this app uses Apple's CloudKit container.

Contact information

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, you may email rmaraujo@me.com.
